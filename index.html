Welcome to JAVR - Java automatic voice recognition.

JAVR is an fully-automated voice recognition software written in Java. It is licensed under GNU General Public License and free for everybody to use, modify and redistribute non-commercially.

Under developement

JAVR is currently under heavy developement and not 100% working yet. At the moment, JAVR is able to understand you and print on screen what you said. I'm working on integration of WolframAlpha, Weather, News and RaspberryPi compatibility.
